Caesar Salad

Recipe for Caesar Salad by Natasha Kravchuk (Natasha's Kitchen)

Classic Caesar Salad with crisp homemade croutons and a light caesar dressing â€“ for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 30 mins

Taken from natashaskitchen.com (January 15, 2019)

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar Salad dressing

Directions

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine all of your ingredients and toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing.
